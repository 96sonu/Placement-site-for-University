Regulation
Overview

A student can participate in the placement process of a company  subject to the following conditions:

1. The cell has confirmed his /her registration.
2. She/he meets the requirements/eligibility criteria specified  a) by the company and Institute guidelines.

Offer of a Job

1. The company would provide the offer letters to the cell and not  to the students directly.
2. When the cell receives an offer letter from a company for a student, it would communicate the same to him/her.

For Scheduling of the Visit of Companies following information from them is required beforehand: (To be filled in Response Sheet and sent back)

1. Compensation package declared.
2. Confirmation of campus visit and preferred dates
Eligibility
Eligibility

All students graduating from the institute in the year 2015-16 are eligible to participate in the placement activities.

*One Student - One offer : Institute has decided to follow one student one offer policy to cater more students at the campus. Once the student is selected and offer is made, he/she stands out of the placement process.

*Terms and Conditions apply.

 Procedure for placements :
23. Placement policy will be uploaded on the college website.
24. Placement Cell will initiate talks with various companies through personal meetings , Emails , telephonically etc.
25. After confirmation containing openings , Response sheet will be sent to companies.
26. Once the response sheet is received stating Profile , Package , Location, Date of Campus etc , the information will then be downloaded to students of respective streams for registration.
27. Once the Number of registration is received , companies will be informed accordingly.
28. Training Sessions will be conducted for registered students.
29. Campus drive then gets conducted.
30. At the completion of drive , results will be declared on the same day or in a day or two.
31. Selected/Shortlisted students and placement co-ordinator will be informed accordingly.
